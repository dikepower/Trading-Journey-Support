## 🔒 Privacy Policy
Your privacy is our priority. Trading Journey is designed so that we never have access to your sensitive financial data.

### 1. Data Ownership
All data you enter into Trading Journey—including trades, account balances, and strategies—is stored locally on your device. We do not host your data on our own servers.

### 2. iCloud Syncing
We use Apple's **CloudKit** to sync your data across your own devices (iPhone, iPad). This process is handled entirely by Apple's secure infrastructure. We cannot see, access, or share your synced data.

### 3. No Tracking
We do not use third-party analytics or tracking cookies. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or any third parties.

### 4. Security
We recommend using the built-in **FaceID/TouchID** lock feature within the app to further protect your data on your device.
